EDBT/ICDT 2027 Call for Workshops

EDBT and ICDT solicit proposals for workshops to be co-located with the 2027 edition of the joint conference whicb will be held in Lille, France. All workshops will be held on April 6th, 2027. Both half-day and full-day workshops are possible.

Submission Guidelines

A workshop proposal should focus on a specific research area within the field of databases and information systems.

Please submit your workshop proposal by e-mail, as a single PDF file, to the workshop co-chairs, Fatemeh Nargesian and Torsten Grust, at edbticdt2027-workshops@cs.uni-tuebingen.de.

Your proposal should not exceed 4 pages and include the following details:

  • Workshop title
  • Preferred workshop duration (full day, half day)
  • Name, affiliation, e-mail, and short biography—including previous experience in organizing workshops or conferences—of the workshop organizers (please identify one organizer as the primary contact)
  • Main goal of the workshop and a list of the workshop's topics
  • Interest to the EDBT/ICDT audience, relation to other workshops at data management venues
  • Publicity plans
  • Tentative list of workshop PC members
  • Expected statistics regarding number of submissions, number of accepted papers, and number of registered participants
  • Statistics for previous editions of the workshop (number of submitted and accepted papers, number of invited talks, number of panels), if applicable
  • Policy for handling conflicts of interest with workshop PC or organizers

The organizers of accepted workshops are expected to assemble a PC, announce and publicize the workshop and its call for papers, solicit submissions, conduct the reviewing process, and prepare the final workshop program. Workshops will benefit from the EDBT/ICDT 2027 organizational infrastructure and receive support regarding registration, badges, lunches, coffee breaks, and online publication of the workshop proceedings.

As in previous years, all workshop papers will be published in free open-access proceedings at CEUR-WS (see https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4192/ for the proceedings of the 2026 edition of the EDBT/ICDT workshops).
